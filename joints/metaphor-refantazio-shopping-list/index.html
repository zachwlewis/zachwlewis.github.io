Mask Crafting Helper

Instructions

This tool helps track the Masks created and the White Masks required to craft every Mask in Metaphor: ReFantazio. As you craft Masks, check them off in the Masks list. This will update the list of Mask Requirements to show the ingredients needed to craft the remaining Masks. Your selections are automatically saved to your local storage, so you can come back to it later.

Some Masks require other Masks to craft. Enabling Show computed ingredients will display the White Masks required to craft all the Masks in the Mask Requirements list.

I'd recommend starting this around 09/26, as you'll have access to most classes and the Wary Shopkeep at Port Brilehaven who will sell White Masks.

This data was pulled from the Metaphor: ReFantazio guide from game8.co.
