Help: Cooking Battle Helper

Judge List

Select the four judges participating in the cooking battle.

You can filter the list with the search bar.

Best Dishes

This will display the calculated best dishes for the four judges selected.

Hovering over a dish will highlight it for all judges.

It is possible that a best dish will not appear in the top judges list because math.

Scorecards

Each judge will have their own scorecard showing their name, favored cusine, and top dishes broken down by course.

Hovering over a dish will highlight it for all judges.
